About Comparely

What Comparely is, how the comparisons work, and where it's headed.

Mission

Buying decisions get harder every year — more models, more marketing, more conflicting reviews. Comparely's mission is simple: put two products side by side and show you, plainly, where each one actually wins. No overall score hiding the trade-offs, no sponsored placement — just price, specs, features, and real pros and cons, broken down category by category.

How Comparely Works

1

Search & select

Search or filter the catalog on the Compare page and add any two products to your comparison tray.

2

We break it down

Comparely lines up price, ratings, features, specifications, and pros & cons — matching spec rows so the comparison stays fair.

3

See the winner

Each category gets a clear winner, plus an overall verdict, so you can decide in seconds instead of a dozen open tabs.

Why Comparely is Different

  • Category-by-category winners, not one vague overall score — you see exactly what you're trading off.
  • Every spec row is compared on equal terms, using the same units and direction, not cherry-picked stats.
  • No sponsored placement. This preview runs on a fixed sample dataset, not paid rankings.
  • Built for two products at a time, on purpose — most real decisions come down to two finalists.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real, live product data?

No. Comparely's current catalog is illustrative sample data for demonstration purposes, not live pricing or inventory.

Can I compare more than two products at once?

Not yet — Comparely is built around focused, two-product comparisons by design. That may expand in the future.

How is the winner in each category decided?

Price and ratings compare directly. Specifications compare like-for-like where both products report a value (battery life, storage, and similar), and the category winner is whichever product wins more of those individual rows. Some specs, like display size or processor name, are shown for reference without declaring a winner, since "better" genuinely depends on what you value.

Does Comparely save my comparisons?

Not in this preview. Selections reset when you close the tab. Saved comparisons are on the roadmap.

Is Comparely Assistant a real AI?

Not yet. The interface is fully built — messages, suggestions, regenerate, stop, and more — but it currently returns preview responses rather than live AI-generated answers.

Privacy

Comparely is a demo application. It doesn't require an account or sign-in, and it doesn't send your searches or comparisons to a server — everything runs in your browser for the length of your visit and resets when you leave. If a future version adds accounts or persistent history, this page will be updated to reflect exactly what's stored and why.
